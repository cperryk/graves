Game of Thrones Graveyard

This is a restoration of the Slate Game of Thrones Graveyard, which ran through the series' final seasons.

As Slate's website has evolved, many old interactive projects became broken, and fixing them all is infeasible. I restored this one just because I was a huge fan of the show, which was notorious for killing characters in unpredictable and brutal fashion, and it was one of my most popular cultural pieces at Slate. This one was the right mix of simple, amusing, and satirical.

Click to leave a flower to mourn your favorite character. Disclaimer: There are no more servers powering this thing anymore. The flowers are now randomly generated.
